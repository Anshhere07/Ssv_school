SSV

Mr.Ritesh Kumar is an accomplished education entrepreneur and the visionary founder of SSV School,tilhar. With a passion for nurturing young minds, they established the preschool in 2020 with a commitment to providing a safe, modern, and engaging learning environment. Their educational philosophy is based on creativity, experiential learning, and strong moral values.

Under their leadership, the school has introduced innovative teaching methods such as Montessori, Play-way, Activity-based learning, Creator, Self-motivator, ensuring children learn through exploration and joy. They believe that every child is unique and strives to create a space where each student feels loved, confident, and encouraged.

Today, SSV School is recognized for its caring staff, structured curriculum, and warm atmosphere—reflecting the owner’s dedication to quality education and community trust.
